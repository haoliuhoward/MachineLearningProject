Auto Parts City, the country's first 700 businesses go eviction

Haidian District, a store which sells auto parts, the government plans to forcibly shut down development in the next year, so that in operation for two decades of small businesses, a lifetime of hard work destroyed. Most businesses take with their families and staff, a total of about 2,000 people Monday (the 15th) strike, and to the district government protest. (Huang Yuetao report)

Haidian District, western suburbs of Auto Parts City 700 businesses, recently notified the mall will be closed in the next year, but the Government has failed to explain the reason for closing the mall and the placement, businesses and staff in conjunction with the families of the 2,000 people on Monday collective strike and protest march to city hall. According to mainland media quoted the district government officials said, the western suburbs of Auto Parts City next year will be the restructuring and development of information industry, and the current staff arrangements Auto Parts City, are still discussing them.

According to Mr. Su's friend merchant, he said he was informed that the government forcibly closed unreasonable Auto Parts City, it made a special trip to come to assist Mr. Su and other merchants. He said the government doing a great impact on 700 small businesses, they live with their families also in this area for many years, was forced to move, can not continue to do business, I do not know what the future plans.

Mr. Su's friend: many businesses are affected very large, a young and old, to go to school to study here is the money. To move where you want to move? So many family can move it?

He also said that the Government is now even no compensation and relocation arrangements, the Government does not have official contact with the merchant, even the government does not know what to do next.

Su friend: No, no, nothing. At present, the government does not know what to do. I learned something, you came here to help a friend.

In addition, businesses Mr. Wang also seem overwhelmed. He said the government will know the next year to close the area, but the government has not talked to the next move with the merchant, but did not mention the issue of compensation to the merchant. Mr. Wang did not participate in strike action, he said, now only waited in vain, do not know how to do.

Wang: I have not yet processed, we are still waiting to be processed, do not know how.

Auto Parts City in the western suburbs of doing business for twenty years Miss Sun said there were part of the business earlier this month to pay rent, but only half of the contracts they sign. There Auto City office staff to the businesses that the Auto Parts City to close and transformation, and will not be rent, and all businesses should be removed before June next year.

Residents living in the vicinity of the district government, said some 2,000 businesses Monday braved the cold weather to the district government protests, the atmosphere generally calm, but the government is still shut the door, merchants get no response from the authorities, police cars and More than armed guard at the scene.

Auto Parts City businesses have said that the government only two months ago had a meeting, orally proposed to relocate the business, but no formal notice and explanation.
