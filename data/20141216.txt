Shenzhen-Shing factory leather clothing thousands of workers went on strike, and hung protest banners at the door. (Workers rights)
Shenzhen, a Hong Kong-owned leather goods company thousand workers continued to strike seven days now, workers refers to the upcoming relocation of the factory, is still not resolved the issue of social security funds and non-payment of workers on strike indefinitely. Another Hong Kong-owned electronics factory in Guangzhou, Elec & Eltek, hundreds of workers went on strike for higher wages. (Highland report)

Shenzhen Mission Hills Qing Sheng Dress Leather Co., about one thousand workers since last Wednesday (10th) onwards, until Tuesday (the 16th) are still on strike. One worker to this station, said a few days ago, the factory posted notice refers, from 8:00 Tuesday morning, the workers to return to work, such as playing cards does not work, regarded as absenteeism, but today the whole factory workers involved in the strike. Factory workers elect representatives to negotiate with the workers' representatives do not choose, because four or five times before the negotiations did not result, the boss did not show up, the workers feel useless. Since the strike, government departments such as the street and the labor bureau staff also arrived at the scene, no results. Today a reporter to the factory to understand, government officials to stop them, they did not shoot.

He also said that four days ago, the factory has moved some of the supplies to the company's Shenzhen Lihua Group, estimated that the factory workers about to be moved to Unilever Group. The factory refused to explain specific date, the factory workers must recover unpaid social security and provident fund in the year before, or after the factory was empty, they would be difficult to recover.

Workers said: that they get back to work this thing (social security, provident fund), we work, he did not get, we did not mind the work. The last thing that he move plants to give us a specific time, and there is no money to pay, so the way he was, I was afraid when he evacuated the Spring Festival this plant.

Reporter had telephoned the Unilever Group of Hong Kong, the staff refers, she did not know this, find someone to reply later, but before the deadline has not yet replied.

Reporters call the Qing Sheng Dress Leather Co., Ltd., Tel no answer.

The workers said earlier that the main requirements of the factory workers reply, when a four-year fund to pay the arrears, as well as long years of non-payment of workers' social security payments. The factory on December 9, said New Year's Day will give a reply, but the workers said it was not specific, requiring corrective specific programs, indicating pay date.

Factory has not for employees to purchase fund since December 2010, in addition to the implementation of the government since 1999 to buy the social security for workers, but the factory start from 2003 for some workers to buy, many long years of social security workers are owed several years.

Unilever Unilever Group, which owns Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Qing Sheng Dress Leather Co., Ltd, Shenzhen Lihua Garment Co., Ltd. and so on a number of IMC clothing garment manufacturing plant.

In addition, Hong Kong Kingboard Chemical Group of Guangzhou Luogang District Elec & Eltek Electronics Factory hundreds of workers on Monday (the 15th) at the factory sit-in strike, and pulling banners ask for an increase of wages in the door, the police presence alert. The strikers put more demands refers holiday shall not buckle double overtime, as well as work clothes, accommodation and meals free of charge, to adjust working hours, five insurance payments range, with equal pay and other benefits.

Informed users Taotao Lu refers to the year again, another strike, probably hundreds of people, requiring more money, probably to see if they add. If the increase in wages, after the New Year continued here do not add, next year will not come to this plant.

Reporter had telephoned Guangzhou Elec & Eltek electronics factory, office and switchboard was no answer. Guangzhou Economic Development Zone Labor Bureau, telephone also no answer.

Reporters then contact the Hong Kong Kingboard Chemical Holdings, staff refers to that they do not interview, the reporter called Elec & Eltek Electronics Factory query.

Elec & Eltek Electronics Factory in 2012 Valentine's Day, more than 2,000 workers strike has also occurred. The electronics factory was established in 1993, mainly produces multi-layer printed circuit board, a staff of more than 2,000 people.
