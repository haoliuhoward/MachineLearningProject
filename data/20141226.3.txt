December 26, 2014, Hebei Langfang City, nearly a thousand investors pull banners, outside city hall to protest against the fraudulent investment companies cheat their money. (Provided by respondents Miss Zhang microblogging)
Langfang City, Hebei Province, nearly a thousand investors, finance companies suspected fraud, recently went to city hall to protest lax regulation, so that their life savings down the drain. Some investors said it had reported to the police, but no progress. (Huang Yuetao report)

To city hall to protest the nearly a thousand investors, namely Langfang City Investment Management Co. and Dennis Jitong Investment Co. customers. Their dissatisfaction with the company claiming lack of funds, but not paid interest to them and can not get back the principal, on Monday (22 May) to the Langfang municipal government outside the containment, to protest the government does not fight for their rights for them. There are unconfirmed sources, there are three female protesters were remanded to municipal police officers, beating induced coma.

Dennis investment company investing in a nearly 21 Miss Wan Dizhang to this station, said the company behind these investments, there is the boss "backing." She also said, Langfang municipal government was chaotic, so that investment company problems. She said, after a friend introduced to the company's investment, in June this year invested $ 100,000 in the company scheduled three months later to give her five percent interest, so she then "overweight" investment of more than twenty thousand, But later the company paid no interest to her.

Miss Zhang: I put 100 000 100 days, the company gave me the interest, so I put 110,000, and now not a penny.

Miss Chang also said that the company invest in front of her, she said, deposits 100 days that is five percent interest; and, there is twelve percent interest on deposits six months; deposits a year, there is one hundred Twenty-four-thirds interest. She said that since this year after September 28, she has not received interest, even if the principal can not even take.

(Provided by respondents Miss Zhang microblogging) Miss Chang said, he has repeatedly asked to meet with the company owner, but the owner of the investment company never showed up. For lack of funds, the company said, Miss Zhang did not believe, and said the company just opened in May this year, the book refers to the company's three million, indicating that no funds may be insufficient.

Miss Zhang: We have to report to the Public Security Bureau, referring to the company did not pay, but the company has 30 million book, just opened more than four months, how could afford it?

Miss Zhang pointed out that the police can not help the rest of the investors, but is an investment company boss "talk a good talk," means that when the boss is not easy, requiring investors to compassionate them.

Another customer also is investing in to this station Dennis Tsang said he invested in the company of three hundred thousand, and now, also can not get back the principal. He also reported to the police, but police said Mr Tsang is a voluntary contract with the investment firm, it is also an event helpless. Mr Tsang said that when the contract expires, they simply did not get the principal, against being lured to sign another contract.

Mr. Tsang: Public Security Bureau said that you (investors) contract is signed voluntarily, no one forced you, but not your company have to sign.
Mr Tsang said the police investigation is now just a case, but the case did not progress until now. Mr Tsang refers now opened a lot of Langfang City Investment Company, we believe Langfang municipal government should supervise investment firms.

This station on the incident to call the Public Security Bureau in Langfang City of inquiry, the Public Security Bureau said that the case is being processed, the specific case is unclear. As for Monday has three female protesters were taken away by police, police said they do not know the situation, saying only that the case will have the latest information available to the public.
